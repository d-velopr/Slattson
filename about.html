What is Slattson ?

Slattson is a thoughtful essentials brand built for everyday wear. We design pieces you actually reach for—soft tees, clean silhouettes, and details that last. Our drops are intentionally small and often available for pre-order so we can reduce waste, keep quality high, and deliver a better fit and feel. It’s simple: fewer pieces, more intention.

Read Our Story

Our Vision

To make premium basics that help people buy less and love what they own. We see a community where comfort, durability, and responsible production all live together—timeless designs, transparent practices, and products that stay in your rotation, not in a landfill.

Our Mission

Craft reliable, responsibly made essentials at fair prices—and keep improving them with your feedback. We prioritize comfort, fit, and longevity; favor small-batch production and lower-impact materials; and back it all with clear sizing, simple care, and responsive support. Every release is built to work hard today and feel even better tomorrow.
